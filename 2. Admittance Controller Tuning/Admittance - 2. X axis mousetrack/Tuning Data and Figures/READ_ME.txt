
% Data decsription
.mat files are data from mousetrack in format: [Time(s), Force(N), Velocity(m/s)]
% Filename
Mxxx indicates the mouse
m0p01_c1 indicates constant mass (m) of 0.01 kg and damping (c) of 1 Ns/m
m0p01_0p1_c_1_1 indicates a guassian mass and damping profile as a function of velocity, 
... with the two values (e.g. 0.01 and 0.1 for mass) indicating the desired and maximum.
